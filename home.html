Why Rewind?

The Importance Of Breaks


As people become busier in their daily lives, many become engulfed in their tasks and forget to take breaks. Forgetting breaks can lead to burnouts and higer stress levels.


Breaks can:
  • Increases productivity
  • Improves mental health and well-being
  • Restores focus and attention, especially for long term goals
  • Can prevent decision fatigue
  • Increases creativity
  • Promotes healthy habits
  • “Rest” helps consolidate memories and improves learning

Introducing the

Pomodoro Technique

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management system that encourages people to work with the time they have—rather than against it. Using this method, you break your working hours into segmented chunks separated by breaks. These intervals are referred to as pomodoros.


The Pomodoro Technique® was invented in the early 1990s by Francesco Cirillo, a developer and entrepreneur. The technique earned its catchy name in honor of the tomato-shaped timer Cirillo used to track his 25-minute intervals of focused work time.

Pomodoro technique is highly effective as it helps you effectively manage your time and work on a task without distractions. It is also beneficial as it helps you become more disciplined and think about your work. This technique is designed to combat multitasking and improve concentration.
